Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza is crispy, cheesy, and loaded with toppings. It's perfect for a fun family dinner or a cozy night in with friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomatoe sauce
  • cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced green bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced green bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onions
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C)
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness and shape
  3. Transfer the dough to a lightly greased pizza pan or baking sheet
  4. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving about 1/2 inch
    of space around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  6. Arrange the black olives, pepperoni, green bell peppers, and red onions on top of the cheese.
  7. Sprinkle the oregano and garlic powder over the toppings./li>
  8. Drizzle the olive oil over the entire pizza.
  9. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
